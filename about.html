About Flavour Dash

Welcome to Flavour Dash, where culinary adventures await! Our story began in the bustling city of Nairobi, Kenya. It all started with a passion for food and a desire to bring people together through exceptional dining experiences.

Flavour Dash was founded in 2024 by two food enthusiasts, Gus and Tasha. Inspired by the vibrant food scene of Nairobi, they set out on a mission to create a platform that celebrates local flavors and connects food lovers with their favorite restaurants.

Our journey has been filled with delicious discoveries and memorable moments. From cozy cafes to five-star restaurants, we've curated a diverse selection of dining options to suit every palate. Whether you're craving traditional Kenyan cuisine or international delicacies, Flavour Dash has something for everyone.
